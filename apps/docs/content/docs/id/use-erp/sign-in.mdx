# Sign in to Afenda ERP

Use your tenant URL and credentials to reach the ERP workspace. Afenda routes authentication through governed auth segments — each path maps to a lane (access, verify, security, workspace, and others).

## Steps

1. Open your tenant sign-in URL (typically `/sign-in` on your tenant host).
2. Enter email and password, or use your organization's SSO provider when enabled.
3. Complete MFA when prompted at `/mfa`.
4. Select a workspace at `/workspace/select` if you belong to more than one.
