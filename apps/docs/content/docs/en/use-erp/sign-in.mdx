## Overview

Use your tenant URL and credentials to reach the ERP workspace. Afenda routes authentication through **governed auth segments** — each URL maps to a lane (access, verify, security, workspace, and others). The flow below is the happy path; your tenant may enable SSO or require MFA on every sign-in.

<Callout type="info" title="Tenant URL">

Your organization admin provides the tenant host (for example `https://your-company.example.com`). Bookmark **`/sign-in`** on that host. Generic marketing URLs do not carry tenant context and will not restore your session.

</Callout>

## Sign-in flow

<Steps>

<Step>

### Open your tenant sign-in URL

Navigate to `/sign-in` on your tenant host. When SSO is enabled, use the provider button configured by your admin instead of email and password.

</Step>

<Step>

### Enter credentials

Sign in with email and password, or complete your organization's SSO flow. If email verification is pending, you may be routed to `/verify-email` before continuing.

</Step>

<Step>

### Complete MFA

When MFA is required, enter the code from your authenticator app at `/mfa`. If you lose device access, use `/mfa/recovery` with a one-time recovery code from your admin.

</Step>

<Step>

### Select a workspace

Users with more than one membership land on `/workspace/select` to choose the company or workspace context for this session. Single-membership users skip this step automatically.

</Step>

</Steps>

## After sign-in

| Situation | Route | What to do |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Session expired | `/session-expired` | Sign in again; unsaved work may be lost |
| Access denied | `/access-denied` | Ask your tenant admin to review role and membership |
| Invite pending | `/invite/accept` | Complete invite acceptance before using ERP modules |
