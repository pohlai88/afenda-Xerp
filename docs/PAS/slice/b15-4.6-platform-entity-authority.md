## Rules frozen

1. `PLATFORM_ENTITY_IDS` lists 13 PAS §4.6 examples + `workspace` + `approval` derived extensions (15 total).
2. Registry is authority-only — no DB queries, migrations, authorization checks, or audit writes.
3. `runtimeStatus` literals: `live` | `derived` | `deferred` via `PLATFORM_ENTITY_RUNTIME_STATUSES`.
4. `persistenceOwner` documents package/runtime owner of persistence — `@afenda/database` for live tables.
5. Workspace and approval remain `derived` with empty `schemaPaths`.
