## Rules frozen

1. Kernel may describe accounting words; it must not execute accounting.
2. All vocabulary is closed-set constants with `is*` narrowers (or governed `parse*` where throw/null semantics exist).
3. Branded accounting IDs live on `@afenda/kernel/erp-domain/accounting` only — never `ID_FAMILIES`.
4. `AccountingDomainWireContext` remains JSON-serializable with compile-time assert.
5. Permission key vocabulary documents registry parity — evaluation stays in `@afenda/permissions`.
6. Consolidation method and chart-of-account structure are contract-only labels — no elimination arithmetic.
7. No Drizzle, posting services, or ledger calculation under `erp-domain/accounting/`.
