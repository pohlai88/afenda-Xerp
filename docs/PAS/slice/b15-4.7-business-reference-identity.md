## Rules frozen

1. Kernel owns only business-reference ID vocabulary (`CustomerId` … `AssetId`) — seven families in `business-reference-id.contract.ts`.
2. Every business-reference family has `owner: kernel` and a non-empty `recordOwner` in `ID_FAMILIES`.
3. Kernel must not own business record behavior, validation, lifecycle, import/export, CRUD, UI, or persistence.
4. Prefer "Business Reference Identity Authority" over "Business Master Data Authority" in new kernel identity prose.
5. `BUSINESS_MASTER_DATA_*` contracts remain exported for backward compatibility — §4.7 policy is the PAS authority name.
6. Business Partner convergence (`BusinessPartnerId`) is deferred — do not add an eighth family in this slice.
